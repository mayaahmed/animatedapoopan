Foreword.

If history were taught in the form of stories, it would never be forgotten - Rudyard Kipling.
Amooma means Grandma and Apoopan means Grandpa in Malayalam. In Kerala, children grow up listening to the many escapades of Amooma and Apoopan. There is a pleasant timeless quality about these simple stories. In this book, I present stories, I heard as a child, from family and friends.
Maya Mohsin Ahmed
