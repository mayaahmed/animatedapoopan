Chapter 15

The Blue Fox.

One day a hungry fox came to Apoopan's village to steal some chickens. But the dogs saw the fox and gave it a chase. The fox entered Apoopan's house to hide. Amooma had added some blue powder to a bucket of water to dip her white laundry. The fox fell in to this bucket of blue water. When the fox came out of the bucket, he had turned a bright blue color.

When the blue fox emerged from Apoopan's house, the dogs got scared and ran away. This gave the fox quite a thrill. He went to the forest and scared all the animals. The blue fox was a mysterious creature no one had seen in the forest before. He boasted of the power to turn animals to dust. He pushed everyone around. Even lions and tigers were scared of the blue fox.

Soon it was a full moon night. All the foxes in the forest began to howl at the moon. The blue fox forgot himself and joined in the howling. This revealed his identity as a fox to the animals of the forest. All the animals gathered around the fox and gave him a good thrashing.

"He could change his appearance but not his nature" said a wise elephant.
