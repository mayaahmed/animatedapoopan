Chapter 8

Shrinking beans

Apoopan planted a bean plant for the first time. It grew plenty of beans. One day Amooma said "Give me some beans to cook."

"Okay" said Apoopan. "But you should not waste a single bean."

"Yes" said Amooma.

Apoopan measured up beans in a glass and gave it to Amooma. When Amooma cooked it, the quantity of beans reduced and became half a glass. When Apoopan saw this, he became angry and kicked up a fuss.

"I will show you how to cook beans without wasting any" said Apoopan. "You women do not know how to cook."

The next day Apoopan cooked a glass of beans and it turned in to half a glass too.

"Beans do shrink in quantity when cooked!" exclaimed Apoopan.

"This teaches you to not jump to conclusions without understanding the full picture" said Amooma.
