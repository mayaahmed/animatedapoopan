Chapter 6

Amooma's poultry farm

Amooma had a thriving chicken farm but she never cooked any chicken for Apoopan. She kept saying "Let the number grow and then we will eat some."

Apoopan had a burning desire to eat chicken. One day he said "There is a goddess living in the Pipal tree. If you cook chicken and offer it to the goddess, your chicken farm will grow by leaps and bounds. You must cook it with great care."

Consequently, Amooma made fried chicken, often, and placed it under the Pipal tree to please the goddess. Apoopan would go and eat it secretly after she left. Amooma's poultry farm shrank rapidly.

"May be the goddess is not pleased with your chicken fry" said Apoopan. "You should try harder and cook better."

One day Amooma decided to wait for the Goddess to appear. She was shocked to see Apoopan eating the fried chicken.

She became very angry. When Apoopan saw her, he tried to run away. Amooma chased him and he climbed a tree. Amooma hit the tree with a pole and Apoopan fell into a well from the tree. "Cheating finally lands you in a well" said Amooma.
