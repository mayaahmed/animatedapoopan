Chapter 16

Forcing fortune to smile

Two crows called Ram-crow and Shyam-crow lived on a tree near Apoopan's house. Ram-crow was honest but very poor. Shyam-crow was rich, arrogant, and greedy.

One evening Ram-crow went to Shyam-crow's nest to watch television. Shyam-crow made a false story that his television was not working and did not invite Ram-crow in to his nest. As soon as Ram-crow left the door, Shyam-crow switched on the television.

Ram-crow could hear the television from outside. He felt hurt. He went and sat on a branch and started swinging on it. Swings are fun and Ram-crow got carried away and rocked the branch too hard. The branch broke, and Ram-crow landed on the ground. A thorn pricked his feet. Ram-crow went over to Apoopan's house and Apoopan gave him an antibiotic pill.

"Eat half a pill now and the other half after a meal" said Apoopan.

Ram-crow went over to the well to get water to drink with his pill. He sat on the boundary of the well and broke his pill in to half. In the process he dropped one half of the pill in to the well. Ram-crow dived in to the well to retrieve it but he came back with a huge chunk of gold.

Soon Ram-crow owned a big screen television and his nest looked prosperous. Shyam-crow became curious about how Ram-crow gained his wealth. So one day Shyam-crow asked Ram-crow about it. Ram-crow told him the whole story.

Shyam-crow then tried to get rich like Ram-crow. He sat on a branch and he started swinging on it hard. The branch would not break. He pecked at it till it broke. He fell on the ground alright but no thorn pricked his feet. He did have a few broken feathers though. He pricked his foot with a thorn himself and then went to Apoopan. Apoopan gave him an antibiotic pill and told him to take half of it right away and half of it after a meal.

Shyam-crow then went and sat on the boundary of the well and broke the pill in to halves. But no half of the pill fell in to the well. So Shyam-crow dropped one half of the pill in the well. He then dived in to the well to retrieve it only to come back with a huge lump of clay in his beak.
