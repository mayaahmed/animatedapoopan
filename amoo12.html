Chapter 12

Yes Boss!

Once Apoopan was invited to a rich Zamindar's feast. The Zamindar was a egomaniac and he liked people to treat him like a king. Most people did just that, for as long he was pleased, they got to eat, drink, and party for free.

Apoopan had a good time at the feast. Soon it was dessert time and delicious payasam (rice pudding) was served. Everyone was eating payasam with great joy when suddenly the Zamindar exclaimed

"Yuck! This Payasam is no good. It is bitter. I cannot eat a drop of it."

Everyone else in the room agreed and they said

"Yes, yes. It is bitter. It is no good."

They stopped eating the payasam with heavy hearts. However, Apoopan continued eating the payasam with great pleasure.

"Don't you find the payasam bitter? How come you are still eating it?" asked the Zamindar.

Apoopan did not want to contradict the Zamindar but he also did not want to miss out on the payasam. So he said with a grin

"I like the bitterness of payasam, Sir."

The Zamindar and his guests laughed a lot at Apoopan's answer. But only Apoopan got to eat payasam that day.
